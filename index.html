MY Favorite Place

Golden Gate Bridge in San Francisco, CA

Alamo Square is a beautiful and centrally located neighborhood in San Francisco that is home to the famous "Painted Ladies" or "Seven Sisters." They are a group of seven similar, colorfully painted Victorians that line the popular park in this district.

There's nothing quite like riding a cable car up and down San Francisco's hills. While they represent the early innovation that helped to make the city famous, the experience of riding a cable car is timeless.

Lombard Street in San Francisco is known as the world's most crooked street. The postcard-like view of Lombard Street has become famous around the world, and many visitors enjoy watching the cars carefully wind down the eight hairpin turns among the beautiful flower beds.
